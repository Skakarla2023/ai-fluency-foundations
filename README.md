## 🧭 The 4D Framework (Core Concept)

The course is built around Anthropic's **4D Framework** for AI fluency:

| D | Concept | What It Covers |
|---|---------|----------------|
| 1️⃣ | **Delegation** | Deciding *what* to give to AI and *how much* control to hand over |
| 2️⃣ | **Description** | Crafting clear, effective prompts to get the best output |
| 3️⃣ | **Discernment** | Critically evaluating AI responses for accuracy and relevance |
| 4️⃣ | **Diligence** | Using AI responsibly, ethically, and safely |
